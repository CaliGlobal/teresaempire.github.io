The Story of Teresa

Hospitality and luxury have been integral parts of Teresa's life since childhood. She has always had an extraordinary interest in understanding the protocols and standards that shape the elements of royal hospitality. This passion led her to pose important questions about the essence of hospitality.

Teresa's character has been shaped by the cultural and historical heritage surrounding her. She has witnessed many occasions that embody the spirit of Arab hospitality, learning how to welcome guests in a unique way that reflects her rich heritage. Over time, TERESA EMPIRE has become a symbol of distinguished hospitality, telling one of the most beautiful human stories that never fade with time.

FOUNDER.->

In light of the ongoing changes and in alignment with the implementation of Saudi Vision 2030, particularly in the tourism sector, we at Castello Group have been committed since our inception to identifying real opportunities and transferring scientific expertise and knowledge to our most valuable asset—our team. We strive to ensure continuous adaptation by investing in human capital, meeting our clients’ needs, achieving our vision, and documenting our mission. Moreover, we aim to share our expertise with our stakeholders through sustainable luxury services, in line with the Kingdom’s strategy for the our Elite Sector.

Our efforts focus on enhancing the hospitality sector’s capabilities while aligning with our group’s sustainable goals. We are confident in our resources and qualifications, enabling us to make a significant impact and achieve a qualitative leap in developing our human capital. This includes exchanging knowledge with investors, experts, and stakeholders to support all the projects we undertake.

Finally, with precision, pride, and reliance on Allah's blessings, coupled with the support of our wise leadership, we continue to move forward. Our ultimate goal is to be among the leading companies in the tourism and hospitality sector by 2023, adhering to performance quality standards and demonstrating our professional competencies and strategy based on integrity and credibility.
